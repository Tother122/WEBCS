Explore Different Species

foxes

Known for being agile and very elegant. Foxes come in different colors the most iconic being the red fox (looks orange). Fun fact: Foxes can make over 40 different sounds.

Tigers

The largest of the big cats, tigers are powerful hunters with striking striped fur. Fun fact: A tiger's roar can be heard as far as three kilometres away.

Penguins

Found in the Southern Hemisphere, penguins are flightless birds that thrive in cold climates. Fun fact: They have nine forms of communication
